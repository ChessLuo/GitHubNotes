### 动画渲染
  ---




* [android-activityAnim:](https://github.com/yipianfengye/android-activityAnim)
    * 五种实现activity动画切换的方式
* [ParticleTextView:](https://github.com/Yasic/ParticleTextView)
    * 一个用粒子动画显示文字的 Android 自定义 View
* [animate:](https://github.com/hitherejoe/animate)
    * An application demoing meaningful motion on Android
* [AndroidViewAnimations:](https://github.com/daimajia/AndroidViewAnimations)
    * Cute view animation collection.
* [HTextView:](https://github.com/hanks-zyh/HTextView)
    * Animation effects to text, not really textview
* [Material-Animations:](https://github.com/lgvalle/Material-Animations)
    * Android Transition animations explanation with examples.
* [FloatingView:](https://github.com/UFreedom/FloatingView)
    * FloatingView can make the target view floating above the anchor view with cool animation
